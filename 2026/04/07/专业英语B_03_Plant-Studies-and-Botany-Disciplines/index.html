Plant Studies and Botany Disciplines

Studying Plants

  • Early human interest in plants first centered around how they provide food + medicine
  • This led to plant study becoming a science (search for knowledge of the natural world)
  • Plant science → scientific method

Scientific Method(General)

  1. Observation
  2. Question
  3. Hypothesis
  4. Experiment
  5. Conclusion
  6. Result

Major Disciplines of Plant Science

Plant Anatomy

  • The study of the internal structure of plants(植物解剖学)
  • Developed during the 1600’s
  • Early plant anatomists discovered different tissues in stems and roots

Plant Physiology

  • The study of plant functions(植物生理学)
  • Developed during the 1600’s
  • Early plant physiologists discovered plant cells have very different functions from animal cells

Plant Taxonomy

  • The study of describing, naming, and classifying organisms in the plant kingdom(植物分类学)
  • Oldest branch of plant study
  • Still thousands of plants that have not yet been described or even discovered

Plant Geogrophy

  • The study of how and why plants are distributed where they are (植物地理学)
  • Developed in the 1800’s

Plant Ecology

  • The study of the interaction of plants with one another and with their environment(植物生态学)
  • Developed in the 1800’s

Plant Morphology

  • The study of the form and structure of plants(植物形态学)
  • Developed during the 1800’s

Genetics

  • The study of heredity(遗传学)
  • Developed in the early 1900’s
  • First experiments began on pea plants
  • Includes plant breeding (improved crop quality and yield)

Genomics

  • Focuses on all genes and their function(基因组学)
  • Impacts nearly all plant genetic research
  • Genetics = individual genes
  • Genomics = all genes together

Bioinformatics

  • Comines biology, statistics, and computer science.(生物信息学)
  • Analyzes data sets from plant DNA sequencing

Cell Biology

  • The study of cell structure and the function of those structures(细胞生物学)
  • The mid 1900’s invention of the electron microscope led to big new discoveries
  • Discovered how cells multiply and how cell internal components work

Economic Botany (Ethnobotany)

  • Studies the practical use of plants and plant products(经济植物学)
  • Focuses on the use of plants for food, fiber, medicines, and other purposes
